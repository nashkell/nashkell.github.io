Art Direction, Branding,
Exhibition Design, Web Design

Led a branding and information design team to prepare for a one-day exhibition of the work for the 2014 Senior Graphic Design class. An identity system was formed based around the quantification of the senior class. The result was a cohesive suite of exhibition graphics and web interface. We started by taking a survey of our work, interests and background and developed a visual system to reflect our collective personalities. The purpose for choosing to represent the students in this light hearted manner was due to the fact that many people will remember a persons face before they will remember their name. This interface provided the opportunity for people to relate a persons personality to their corresponding portfolio.
